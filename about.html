About

Hi, I’m Pia, a UX/UI designer, with a background in Graphic Design. I am most known for my approach in solving challenging problems by analyzing patterns in behaviors and motives while paying close attention to details.
Curious by nature, I love thinking outside the box to find innovative techniques that connect people with great designs. UX allows me to design solutions that directly correlate with an improved lifestyle. It places the user’s needs first and foremost with the initial aim to help them reach their goal.

My background in Graphic Design has made balancing human centered design, sustainable practices and business needs, a key strategy in my approach. Ultimately, with having lived and and worked on three different continents, I aim to create solutions that hit home for every user.

For questions or if you would like to see my résumé please email me.
